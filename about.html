About

Better Professor is an app that takes the stress out of mentoring your graduate students. Designed and built with higher education in mind, Better Professor gives you the tools to simplify your day-to-day tasks as a mentor. Help students stay on track with their project deadlines and remember to provide valuable feedback with Better Professor so you can guide their academic success.

Forgetting about deadlines is a thing of the past. Better Professor makes staying up to date with your students easy, giving you the freedom to concentrate on your own academic work.
